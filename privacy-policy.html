1. Information I Collect

I do not collect or store any personal information on servers. Any data such as in-game names you enter is saved locally in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to me or any third parties.

I do not use backend servers or databases to collect or store your personal information.

My websites do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

2. Use of DNS and Hosting Services

I use Cloudflare as my DNS provider and GitHub Pages to host my websites. These services may collect minimal technical data as part of their normal operation, but I do not have access to any personal data collected by them.

I recommend reviewing their privacy policies:

  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Privacy Policy

3. Cloudflare CAPTCHA and Security Measures

To protect my websites from abuse and malicious traffic, Cloudflare may present CAPTCHAs or other security challenges to visitors. These are controlled by Cloudflare and may collect minimal data (e.g., IP addresses, browser information). I do not have access to the detailed data collected by Cloudflare.

For more information, please review Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Liability Disclaimer: I am not liable for Cloudflare's security measures such as CAPTCHAs or bot checks that visitors may encounter. These measures are independently implemented by Cloudflare.

4. Your Privacy Rights

Since I do not collect or store any personal data, you have full control over the information saved in your browser.

You can clear or delete the data stored locally at any time via your browser settings.

5. Liability Disclaimer

I am not liable for any personal information such as real names, phone numbers, or other sensitive data that you may choose to enter as in-game names or other inputs saved locally in your browser.

Please avoid entering sensitive or personally identifiable information in any input fields.

6. Multiplayer Data and Visibility

Since I offer a multiplayer game, any in-game names or data you enter may be visible to other players in real-time. Please be cautious not to enter sensitive personal information.

I am not responsible for any personal information that you choose to share or display within the game.

7. Use of WeatherAPI.com

My websites may offer weather-related features using the external service WeatherAPI.com. When you enter a location (such as a city name or ZIP code), this data is sent directly to WeatherAPI.com to retrieve weather information.

I do not store or log the locations you enter. The request is made from your browser directly to WeatherAPI.com, and any information returned (such as temperature or weather conditions) is used only to display weather results in the interface.

Please refer to WeatherAPI.com's Privacy Policy for details about how they handle location data and API usage.

8. Third-Party Links

My websites may contain links to other websites. I am not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

9. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I encourage you to review it periodically. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.

10. Contact Me
